01ABOUT THE PRACTICE

NKY Mechanical Designs is an independent mechanical engineering consulting practice serving Cincinnati, Northern Kentucky, and clients across the United States. We help small and mid sized manufacturers, product companies, and inventors move complex ideas from concept to production with senior engineering firepower on demand.

Founded by Matthew E. Grubbs, a BSME with twenty eight years of hands on product design, tooling, qualification testing, and manufacturing engineering across defense, aerospace, automotive Tier 1, and consumer product companies. 02SERVICES

> Product Design & Development

Concept through production. 3D CAD in Creo, SolidWorks, Solid Edge, Inventor, and CATIA. GD&T detailed drawings to ASME Y14.5. Design for manufacturing with domestic and international contract manufacturers.

> Tooling, Fixtures & SPM

Production tooling, jigs, gauges, and special purpose machinery. Access and clearance studies. 2D drawings and 3D models ready for fabrication and test. Full design evolution from concept through delivery.

> FEA & Simulation

Structural, modal, random vibration, shock, and thermal analysis in Creo Simulate and Inventor FEA. Load cases, boundary conditions, and acceptance criteria that stand up to design review.

> Qualification & DV Testing

Design verification and qualification per MIL-STD-810 thermal, vibration, and shock. Test plan authoring, execution support, and root cause failure analysis using 8D, 5 Whys, DFMEA, and PFMEA.

> Automation & Robotics

PLC programming on Allen Bradley and Mitsubishi platforms. Robotic cell design and integration with Fanuc, Motoman, and Panasonic. Vision systems, poka yoke fixturing, and Lean cell layout.

> Prototyping & Fabrication

Quick turn, low cost proof of concept prototypes in additive (FDM, SLA), CNC machining, sheet metal, and welded assemblies. Iterate fast to validate design intent before you commit to tooling.

> Patent & IP Support

Invention disclosure authoring, freedom to operate analysis, prior art review, and technical drawing support for provisional and utility filings. Six granted and fifteen filed patents of experience.

> Reverse Engineering

Legacy and obsolete parts brought back to life. 3D scan to CAD model to fabricated replacement. Ideal for line down emergencies and long tail spare parts that vendors no longer support.
